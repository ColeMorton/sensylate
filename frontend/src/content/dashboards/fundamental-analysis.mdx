# Fundamental Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive dashboard for analyzing stock fundamentals including:

- Revenue and free cash flow trends
- Business segment breakdown
- Geographic revenue distribution
- Quality metrics and ratings
- Financial health indicators
- Investment pros and cons
- Valuation methodologies
- Balance sheet analysis

The dashboard provides a complete view of a company's financial health and investment potential.
